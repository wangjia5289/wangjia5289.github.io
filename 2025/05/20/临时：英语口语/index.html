临时：英语口语

Last updated on 2025-05-20T11:11:10+08:00

Unit1

  1. Describe your favorite natural or man-made wonder.
  2. Discuss: Which place would you most like to visit? Why?

Unit2

  1. Describe your ideal job.
  2. Discuss: What qualifications are needed to become a good teacher? Why?

Unit3

  1. Describe your favorite sport or activity.
  2. Discuss: Talk about the ways to stay active.

Unit4

  1. Describe your favorite high-tech product/products.
  2. Discuss: What are the advantages and disadvantages of AI?

Unit5

  1. How do you get news and information? Describe your ways.
  2. Discuss: “Great minds discuss ideas. Average minds discuss events. Small minds discuss people.”
    –Eleanor Roosevelt
    Do you agree with this quote? Why?

Unit 1 最喜欢的奇观
My favorite wonder is the Great Barrier Reef in Australia. It’s vast and colorful, stretching over two thousand kilometers. I love snorkeling there because I can swim among bright fish and graceful sea turtles. It feels like exploring a hidden rainbow world beneath the waves. The coral formations shift in shape and hue with the sunlight, creating an ever-changing panorama. Plus, seeing local conservation efforts gives me hope for the reef’s future. I always leave feeling both humbled and inspired.
我最喜欢的奇观是澳大利亚的大堡礁。它广阔而绚丽，绵延两千多公里。我喜欢在那里浮潜，因为我可以在色彩斑斓的鱼儿和优雅的海龟之间畅游。那感觉就像是在海浪下探索一个隐藏的彩虹世界。珊瑚在阳光的照射下不断变换形状和色彩，呈现出一幅幅不断变化的画卷。而且，当地的保护工作让我对这片礁群的未来充满希望。每次离开时，我总是既感到谦卑又受到启发。

Unit 2 理想的工作
My ideal job is being a travel writer and photographer. It’s adventurous and creative, combining words and images. I love traveling to remote places because I can discover new cultures and taste exotic foods. It feels like living in a storybook every day, full of unexpected chapters. I would spend mornings interviewing locals and afternoons editing photos in a cozy café. Plus, sharing my experiences online connects me with curious readers around the world. I imagine ending each day with a fresh idea for the next destination.
我理想的工作是成为一名旅行作家兼摄影师。这份工作充满冒险和创意，把文字与图像完美结合。我喜欢前往偏远的地方旅行，因为我可以发现新的文化，品尝异国的美食。每天都像是生活在一本充满未知章节的童话书里。我会在早上采访当地人，下午则在温馨的咖啡馆里修图。而且，通过网络分享我的经历，可以让我与世界各地好奇的读者建立联系。我想象着每天的结束，都是为下一站冒险酝酿新点子的开始。

Unit 3 最喜欢的运动或活动
My favorite activity is rock climbing. It’s challenging and exhilarating, both physically and mentally. I love finding new routes because I can test my strength and focus with every grip. It feels like solving a vertical puzzle against gravity. The sound of chalk on my hands and the view from halfway up keep me fully present. Plus, reaching the top brings a burst of pride and excitement that’s hard to match. Climbing also means great company—belaying friends become like a second family.
我最喜欢的活动是攀岩。这项运动既具挑战性又令人兴奋，考验身体也考验心理。我喜欢寻找新的路线，因为每一次攀爬都是对力量与专注的考验。那感觉就像在对抗重力、解一道垂直的谜题。手上的镁粉声和半山腰的风景让我全身心投入。而且，登顶的那一刻，总能带来一种无与伦比的自豪与激动。攀岩也意味着好伙伴——保护你的朋友会像第二个家庭一样重要。

Unit 4 最喜欢的高科技产品
My favorite gadget is my smartwatch. It’s compact and powerful, balancing form and function. I love checking health stats because I can track my exercise and heart rate at a glance. It feels like having a personal coach on my wrist, nudging me to move more. The GPS map guides my runs without carrying a phone, and quick replies keep me connected in a snap. Plus, the battery lasts nearly a week, so I can focus on life instead of chargers. It’s a tiny tech-wonder that makes daily routines smoother.
我最喜欢的高科技产品是我的智能手表。它小巧而强大，兼顾外观与实用性。我喜欢查看健康数据，因为我可以一眼看到自己的运动量和心率。那感觉就像腕上有一个私人教练，时刻提醒我多动一动。内置的 GPS 能在跑步时为我导航，无需携带手机；快捷回复功能也让我迅速与他人保持联系。而且，电池几乎能用上一整周，让我可以专注于生活，而不用总想着充电。它是个小小的科技奇迹，让我的日常变得更加顺畅。

Unit 5 获取新闻和信息的方式
My usual way to get news is using a smartphone app. It’s fast and convenient, with headlines updated in real time. I love browsing those headlines each morning because I can catch up on world events in seconds. It feels like carrying a mini newspaper in my pocket wherever I go. Later in the day, I read a weekly newsletter that summarizes tech and culture stories I care about. Plus, I unwind in the evening with a current-affairs podcast, turning my walk into a learning session. This mix keeps me informed without feeling overwhelmed.
我平时获取新闻的方式是使用手机应用程序。这种方式快捷又方便，头条新闻会实时更新。我喜欢每天早上浏览这些头条，因为可以在几秒钟内了解世界大事。那感觉就像口袋里随时带着一份迷你报纸。到了下午，我会读一份每周的电子简报，上面总结了我感兴趣的科技和文化新闻。晚上散步时，我会听一档时事播客，让散步变成学习时间。这样搭配下来，我既能保持信息通畅，又不会被压得喘不过气来。
